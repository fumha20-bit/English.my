About This Website
English · ትግርኛ is a free learning website for Tigrinya speakers who want to learn English.

📚 What you can learn here

This website has 50 key English vocabulary words across 4 topics: Job & School, Relationships, Vacation, and Concepts. Every word has a Tigrinya translation, a clear English explanation, an example sentence, and a pronunciation button.

There are also 150 real sentences from daily life — with Tigrinya translation and audio for every sentence.

🎯 How to study

1. Start with Vocabulary Cards — open each word and press 🔊 to hear it. Repeat out loud.

2. Go to Flashcards — test yourself. Mark words as learned.

3. Read the Sentences — listen to them and try to understand.

4. Take the Quiz — choose the correct meaning for each word.
